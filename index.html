For His Kingdom Co

For His Kingdom Collective is a community of believers, artists, storytellers, worshippers, and leaders united by one purpose: to make Jesus Christ known and to raise up a generation of saints.


We exist to create spaces, through media, music, testimony, formation, and mission, where people encounter the truth, love, and power of God.


We believe creativity is a weapon, testimony is a ministry, and evangelization is our mandate.


Everything we do — retreats, worship nights, films, courses, outreach — is driven by the desire to bring souls back to Jesus and to boldly proclaim His victory.


We are ordinary people with a supernatural call: To build His Kingdom, one story at a time.

“To forge an army of saints by proclaiming the truth and victory of Jesus Christ, through bold and faithful evangelization of the Gospel, and by the word of our testimony.”
Revelation 12:11
